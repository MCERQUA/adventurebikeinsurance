# Topical Map — adventurebikeinsurance.com

**Brand:** Contractors Choice Agency · **Niche:** Insurance for adventure / dual-sport motorcycle owners (BMW GS, KTM Adventure, Triumph Tiger, Yamaha Ténéré; ADV touring, off-road/dual-sport, accessory & luggage coverage, total-loss replacement, roadside/touring).

## Data note / SEO strategy
Real DataForSEO pulls (US, 2026-06) confirm the exact-match ADV niche is tiny: "adventure motorcycle insurance" ~40/mo, "dual sport motorcycle insurance" ~10/mo, "adv motorcycle insurance"/"bmw gs insurance" ~0. Demand lives in the **broad parent terms** ("motorcycle insurance" 40.5K, "motorcycle insurance quote" 40.5K, "cheap motorcycle insurance" 12.1K, "how much is motorcycle insurance" 8.1K) and adjacent **off-road/dirt-bike** terms ("dirt bike insurance" 1.3K, "off road motorcycle insurance" 170). Strategy: rank the homepage/pillar for the broad money terms with an ADV angle, then **own the low-competition long-tail** (dual-sport, ADV, brand-bike, accessory/total-loss) where there is little competition and high purchase intent. CPCs are high ($30–$115), so commercial intent is strong and lead value is excellent.
